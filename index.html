Jonathon Goins

Your Guide

I'm Jonathon Goins, and waterfowl hunting is what I do. Not as a hobby, as a way of life. For over 15 years, I've been in the timber before sunrise, setting decoys in frozen fields, and reading the weather like it's the only language that matters.

The Roost Hole isn't a corporate operation. It's me, the land I know around Stuttgart, and the birds that work it. I don't promise limits. I promise honest hunting, the kind where you earn it, respect it, and remember it.

We hunt flooded rice fields when the ducks are working the crops. We hunt field spreads when the geese are flying. We go where the birds go, and we do it right: small groups, proven setups, and no shortcuts.

If you want a real hunt with a guide who lives this life year-round, let's talk. No sales pitch. Just straight shooting.
