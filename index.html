Your Guide

I'm Jonathon Goins, and waterfowl hunting is what I do. Not as a hobby, as a way of life. For over 15 years, I've been in the timber before sunrise, setting decoys in frozen fields, and reading the weather like it's the only language that matters.

The Roost Hole isn't a corporate operation. It's me, the land I know around Stuttgart, and the birds that work it. I don't promise limits. I promise honest hunting, the kind where you earn it, respect it, and remember it.

We hunt flooded rice fields when the ducks are working the crops. We hunt field spreads when the geese are flying. We go where the birds go, and we do it right: small groups, proven setups, and no shortcuts.

If you want a real hunt with a guide who lives this life year-round, let's talk. No sales pitch. Just straight shooting.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring on the hunt?
Bring your shotgun, shells (steel shot required), hunting license, warm waterproof clothing, and waders. We provide decoys, blinds, and calling.
Do you offer morning and afternoon hunts?
Most hunts are morning hunts when duck activity is highest. We start well before sunrise. Afternoon hunts may be available depending on conditions.
How do I book a hunt?
Call or text 870-830-3633 to check availability. We'll discuss dates, group size, and hunt preferences. Book early as peak season fills fast.
What's the cancellation policy?
Weather happens. If conditions are unsafe or unfavorable for hunting, we'll work with you to reschedule. Call us to discuss specific situations.
Is lodging included?
Lodging is available for an additional fee. Call 870-830-3633 for availability and rates. We can accommodate multiple hunters comfortably.
