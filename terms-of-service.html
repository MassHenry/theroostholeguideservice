3. Requirements for Hunters

3.1 Legal Requirements

All hunters must have:

  • Valid Arkansas hunting license
  • Federal migratory bird hunting stamp (duck stamp)
  • Arkansas waterfowl stamp
  • HIP (Harvest Information Program) certification
  • Hunter education certificate (if born after January 1, 1969)
  • WMA permit (if hunting on Wildlife Management Areas)

3.2 Age Requirements

  • Hunters under 16 must be accompanied by an adult
  • Youth hunters under 16 without hunter education must hunt with a licensed adult age 21 or older

3.3 Physical Requirements

Hunting requires:

  • Ability to walk in uneven terrain and muddy conditions
  • Ability to enter and exit blinds safely
  • Ability to sit/stand for extended periods
  • Ability to handle firearms safely

Please inform us of any physical limitations or health conditions that may affect your ability to hunt safely.

4. What to Bring

Hunters must provide:

  • Shotgun (12 or 20 gauge recommended)
  • Steel shot ammunition (non-toxic shot required by law)
  • Appropriate hunting clothing (camouflage, waterproof)
  • Waders or hip boots
  • Hunting license and stamps (listed above)

We provide:

  • Decoys and decoy setup
  • Blinds
  • Calling services
  • Field knowledge and guiding

5. Safety and Conduct

5.1 Safety First

IMPORTANT: Hunting involves inherent risks. Your safety is our priority, but you must follow all safety rules and guide instructions.

5.2 Required Safety Practices

  • Follow all firearms safety rules

  • Know your shooting zones and safe firing directions
  • Never point a firearm at another person

  • Wear hunter orange when required

Violation of these rules may result in immediate termination of your hunt without refund.
